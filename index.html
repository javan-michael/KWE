Empowering Our Community

Native Oils is more than just wellness; it’s a social movement. We cultivate our chia seeds right at our village home. By keeping the farming, harvesting, and distillation completely local, we provide meaningful employment and financial independence to the incredible women in our community who nurture these plants from soil to seed.
